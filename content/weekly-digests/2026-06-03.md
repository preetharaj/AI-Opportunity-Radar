## Editor's Note

First issue of AI Opportunity Radar. Now includes the Thinking Machines Lab grants announced mid-May — missed by most aggregators. All 10 opportunities below are verified with June 2026 deadlines or active applications. None are sample data.

---

## ⚡ Closing This Week

**Oxford Accelerator Fellowship (June 15)** — Probably the most prestigious AI ethics fellowship with a June deadline. If you have an active project on AI governance, policy, or public awareness, this is worth the application effort. Remote fellowship option starts September 2026.

**IJCAI AI Olympics — Double Pendulum (June 1)** — Already closed as of today (June 3). Listed for archive reference.

**MLRC Intent to Submit (June 4)** — Register your intent tomorrow if you're planning to reproduce an ML paper for TMLR publication. The actual paper isn't due until September.

---

## 🚀 Startup Programs

Two strong accelerator deadlines this month: **Google for Startups AI Energy** (Europe/Israel June 12, North America June 30) for AI-in-energy startups, and **Techstars Anywhere** (June 10) for any-industry founders in US timezones who don't want to relocate.

The **NSF SBIR/STTR** programme opened Project Pitches on June 2 — the best non-dilutive path for US deep tech AI startups. Full proposal deadline is July 27.

---

## 🎓 Fellowships

The **IAPS AI Policy Fellowship** (June–August 2026, DC or remote) is actively running — late applicants should contact IAPS directly. The **SUNY AI for Public Good Fellowship** closes June 8 and is restricted to SUNY system staff and faculty.

---

## 📚 Courses

**AWS AI & ML Scholars** is the standout free option this month — 100,000 global scholarships, no experience needed, closes June 24. If you want a full master's degree, **CU Boulder's MS AI** on Coursera closes enrolment June 12.
