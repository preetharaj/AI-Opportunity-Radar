The CSIRO Next Generation Graduates Program (NGGP) is Australia's flagship cohort-based, industry-driven AI graduate research training programme. Run by CSIRO Data61, it funds Honours, Masters, and PhD students working on AI, quantum computing, and emerging technology challenges.

**Programme structure:**
- 38 interdisciplinary research programmes across Australian universities
- Students work with CSIRO Chief Investigators and university supervisors simultaneously
- Cohort-based: students connect with peers across disciplines and locations
- Preliminary coursework helps students from non-CS backgrounds gain AI proficiency

**Funding:**
- Honours and Masters (coursework): stipend + top-up + allowances
- Master of Philosophy: A$40,500/year stipend + 2% annual indexation (up to 2 years)
- PhD: higher stipend rate (contact CI for current figures)
- Full tuition support varies by programme and university

**How to apply:**
1. Browse the 38 programmes at csiro.au/NGGP
2. Download the programme brochure to check eligibility
3. Contact Chief Investigators directly using links on their profiles
4. Email Data61-NextGenGrad@csiro.au for general enquiries

**Research areas include:**
- AI in mental health and healthcare
- AI for food systems and sustainability
- Quantum AI
- Trustworthy AI and privacy
- Computer vision and robotics
- AI for climate and energy systems

**Deadlines:**
- Rolling — places filled as they become available throughout 2026
- MPhil final applications close December 31, 2026 for 2027 start

**Note:** Eligibility restricted to Australian/NZ citizens and permanent residents.
