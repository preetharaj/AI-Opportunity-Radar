AWS and Udacity are sponsoring 100,000 learners worldwide with free AI/ML training in 2026. The programme is designed for beginners — no prior technical knowledge needed.

**Programme phases:**

**Phase 1 — Challenge (FREE, closes June 24, 2026):**
- Self-paced online course
- Build AI apps with AWS PartyRock and Amazon Bedrock
- Complete 2 hands-on projects
- Earn a certificate of completion
- Receive 3 months free AWS Skill Builder subscription

**Phase 2 — Nanodegree (top completers only):**
- Full Udacity AI/ML Nanodegree scholarship
- Advanced curriculum and career support

**Three course tracks available:**
1. AWS AI Practitioner Challenge
2. Generative AI fundamentals
3. Applied ML on AWS

Enrol any time before June 24, 2026 — programme is fully self-paced. Note: spots are capped at 100,000 total enrolments.
