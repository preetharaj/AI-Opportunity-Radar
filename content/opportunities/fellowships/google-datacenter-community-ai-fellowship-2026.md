The Google Data Center Community AI Fellowship, powered by Watson Institute in partnership with Google, is designed for community leaders and entrepreneurs using AI to solve local challenges in regions where Google Data Centers operate.

**Programme structure (16 weeks, fully virtual):**
- Virtual immersive: September 1–11, 2026
- Weekly sessions through January 14, 2027
- One-on-one mentorship from practitioners with deep technical expertise
- Integration of Google AI products, tools, and resources
- Financial tools and access to a global investor/grantmaker network

**What fellows receive:**
- High-quality entrepreneurship training with embedded AI skills development
- Committed mentorship from technical experts
- Access to Google's AI products and resources
- Long-term alumni support and community

**Key dates:**
- Priority deadline: July 12, 2026
- Final decisions announced: August 3, 2026
- Programme begins: September 1, 2026

Apply via the Watson Institute application portal. Shortlisted candidates are invited to a 20-minute online interview.
