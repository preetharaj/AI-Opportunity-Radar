The Interledger Foundation Fellowship Program is a 12-month initiative supporting individuals building solutions to expand access to digital financial systems for underserved and unbanked populations globally. The foundation's focus is on open financial infrastructure — removing the technical, regulatory, and community barriers that prevent people from participating in the digital economy.

**What fellows work on:**
- Open payment infrastructure and interoperability
- AI-powered tools for financial inclusion
- Policy and advocacy for equitable digital finance
- Community education and adoption programs
- Developer tooling for open financial standards

**Who is eligible:**
- Researchers, developers, educators, and advocates
- Working on digital financial inclusion or related challenges
- Open to all backgrounds — proposals evaluated on merit and impact potential
- Global — no geographic restriction

**What the fellowship provides:**
- Funding for a 12-month project (amount commensurate with proposal scope)
- Mentorship from Interledger Foundation network
- Global exposure and community connections
- Platform to turn proposals into real-world impact

**Key dates:**
- Application deadline: **June 15, 2026** (11:59 PM EDT)
- Programme duration: 12 months from acceptance

Apply directly through the Interledger Foundation's official fellowship page. Verify current funding amounts and application requirements before submitting.
