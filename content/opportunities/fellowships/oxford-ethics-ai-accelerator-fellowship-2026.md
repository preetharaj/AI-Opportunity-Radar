The Oxford Institute for Ethics in AI Accelerator Fellowship supports academics and professionals advancing ethical, responsible, and human-centred AI. Fellows receive full Oxford affiliation, research support, and access to one of the world's leading AI ethics networks.

**Fellowship options:**
- **Remote fellowship:** Available for fellows based outside Oxford
- **In-person fellowship:** Based at the Oxford Internet Institute; visa required — apply 6 months in advance
- Earliest start date for 2026/27 remote fellowships: September 2026

**Who should apply:**
- Researchers, policy professionals, and practitioners at any career stage
- Working on ethics, governance, safety, or responsible AI deployment
- From any country — global applications welcome

**⚠️ DEADLINE ALERT:**
The original deadline was June 15, 2026. Updated June 9 2026: Applications now close **June 12, 2026 at 23:59 UK time** due to high application volume. Apply immediately.
