The SUNY AI for the Public Good Fellowship places fellows across the academic year, working on AI initiatives that benefit communities, students, and public institutions.

**Programme focus:**
- AI in education and student success
- Ethics and responsible AI in public systems
- Interdisciplinary AI applications

**Eligibility:** SUNY system faculty and staff only.

**Apply:** Via the SUNY Fellows Programs Application Portal — deadline June 8, 2026.
