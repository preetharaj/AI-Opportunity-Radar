The Fulbright-Nehru Doctoral Research Fellowship allows Indian PhD scholars to spend 6–9 months conducting dissertation research at leading US universities and research institutions. The fellowship is jointly funded by the US and Indian governments through USIEF.

**Priority research areas (2026):**
- Artificial Intelligence and related fields
- Energy security
- Space science
- Defense and security technologies

**What's covered:**
- J-1 visa support
- Monthly stipend for living expenses
- Round-trip travel
- Health insurance
- Academic support and orientation

**Eligibility requirements:**
- Enrolled in a PhD programme at an accredited Indian university
- Coursework substantially complete (ABD preferred)
- Must identify and secure a US host institution/supervisor
- Research proposal must fall within priority areas

**Application closes July 1, 2026.** Apply through USIEF's official portal.
