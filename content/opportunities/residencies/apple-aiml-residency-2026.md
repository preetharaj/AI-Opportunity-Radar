The Apple AIML Residency is a one-year paid programme for advanced-degree graduates and experienced engineers to work embedded within Apple's machine learning research and product teams.

**What residents do:**
- Join an Apple team for one year on high-impact ML projects
- Work on features and research that ship into Apple products
- Publish at premier research venues (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.)
- Attend Apple-led and external technical and leadership courses
- Pursue independent study alongside product work
- Partner with research and development teams across Apple

**Who is eligible:**
- Master's degree graduates
- Recent PhD graduates and postdocs
- Software and hardware engineers wanting to collaborate with ML/research teams
- Academics seeking industry sabbatical
- Aspiring professors seeking industry experience
- STEM fields broadly — interdisciplinary collaboration is a stated programme goal

**How to apply:**
- Search `#AIMLresidency` on jobs.apple.com
- Multiple roles across different ML domains listed
- Applications reviewed on a rolling basis

**Deadline note:** No fixed public deadline listed for 2026. Based on prior years (2024: closed Jan 2024, 2025: closed Jan 2025), expect the window to close January 2027. Apply early — roles fill before formal closure.

**Location:** In-person at Apple offices (USA).
