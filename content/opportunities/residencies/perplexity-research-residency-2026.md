The Perplexity Research Residency is Perplexity AI's flagship programme for bringing exceptional research talent from any academic or professional discipline into frontier AI research. Unlike most residencies, it explicitly welcomes non-traditional AI backgrounds.

**Programme structure:**
- Duration: 3 months (some roles offer 6-month term)
- Location: In-person at San Francisco or Palo Alto office — no remote option
- Format: Full-time research embedded with Perplexity's AI team

**Compensation and benefits:**
- $220,000/year annualized base salary (prorated to residency term — ~$55K for 3 months, ~$110K for 6 months)
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) plan
- Visa and immigration sponsorship for international candidates

**Who should apply:**
- Researchers, engineers, analysts from any rigorous discipline
- Theoretical physicists, cognitive scientists, quants, mathematicians, biochemists, philosophers
- No PhD required — strong analytical ability and technical depth in your field are what matter
- Must be ready to start within 8 weeks of submitting application

**Research focus areas:**
- Realtime multimodal AI systems
- Open web agents and autonomous research
- Novel methods for information retrieval and reasoning
- Frontier problems at the boundary of Perplexity's product and research

**Interview process:**
1. Technical assessment
2. Research discussion
3. Collaborative problem-solving with potential mentors
4. Cultural fit and values conversation

**Application:** Rolling — applications reviewed continuously. Apply at perplexity.ai/hub/ai-research-residency when ready to start within 8 weeks.
