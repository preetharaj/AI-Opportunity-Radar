The USAII Global AI Hackathon 2026 is a structured virtual competition designed for students at all levels — from high school through doctoral programmes. No advanced coding required: this is a zero-to-low code competition where AI tools are explicitly permitted.

**⚠️ Registration closes June 6, 2026 — today.**

**Competition structure (5 stages):**
1. Registration (closes **June 6**)
2. Learning sprint
3. Qualifier — June 7–10 (30-min no-code test)
4. Results announced June 12
5. Final build week: **June 14–21** — submit on Devpost by June 21, 11:59 PM ET

**Prize pool:**
- 3 categories × $5,000 each = **$15,000 total**
- Winners announced June 27

**Tracks:** Community challenges, learning, and environment

**Teams:** 2–5 members. Core sessions recorded and available asynchronously across time zones.
