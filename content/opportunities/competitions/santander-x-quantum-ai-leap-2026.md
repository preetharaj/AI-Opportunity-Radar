The Santander X Global Challenge | The Quantum AI Leap is a global startup competition developed by Banco Santander in collaboration with IBM, Bluzec, and Oxentia Foundation. It targets startups and scaleups developing innovative solutions at the intersection of quantum computing and artificial intelligence.

**Three challenge tracks:**
1. **Quantum systems enablement** — Hardware and AI tools that enhance quantum performance and stability
2. **Quantum + AI applications** — Software, algorithms, and real-world applications in finance, energy, logistics, and healthcare
3. **Quantum-safe cybersecurity** — Technologies protecting digital systems against future quantum threats

**What winners receive:**
- **Cash prizes:** €10,000 per winning startup (×3) + €30,000 per winning scaleup (×3) = €120,000 total
- **Santander X 100 community:** Access to Santander's elite global network of high-potential innovators
- **IBM support:** Strategic mentoring, AI sessions, quantum innovation team access, opportunity to pitch IBM Ventures
- **Bluzec services:** Up to €10,000 in quantum training, expert support, collaboration and investment network access

**Eligible countries:** Germany, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, USA, Spain, Mexico, Portugal, United Kingdom, Uruguay

**Timeline:**
- Application deadline: June 30, 2026
- Finalist announcement: July 2026
- Final presentations and winner selection: August/September 2026

Apply via the Santander X platform at santanderx.com.
