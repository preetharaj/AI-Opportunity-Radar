Google for Startups is running its 2026 AI accelerator focused on energy transition — one of the fastest-growing sectors for AI deployment. Cohorts of 10–15 startups work through a mix of remote and in-person sessions over 10 weeks.

**What you get:**
- Up to $350,000 in Google Cloud credits
- 1:1 mentorship from Google engineers
- Sprint projects on your top technical challenges
- Product design and customer acquisition workshops
- No equity taken

**Focus areas this cohort:**
- AI for grid modernisation and enhancement
- Demand flexibility and optimisation
- Energy affordability for households and industries

**Deadlines:**
- Europe & Israel: **June 12, 2026**
- North America: **June 30, 2026**

Apply via the Google for Startups Accelerator page — select the relevant regional programme.
