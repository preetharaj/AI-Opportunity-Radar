NSF SBIR/STTR is the premier non-dilutive funding programme for US deep tech startups. Funds are awarded as grants — no equity taken.

**2026 Timeline:**
- Project Pitch submissions opened: **June 2, 2026**
- Full proposal deadline: **July 27, 2026**

**Funding stages:**
- Phase I: Up to $305,000 (6–12 months, proof of concept)
- Phase II: Up to $1,250,000 (24 months, prototype to product)

**AI-relevant topic areas:** Machine learning systems, NLP, computer vision, robotics, AI for scientific discovery, AI safety and reliability.

**Note:** A Project Pitch (short pre-proposal) is required before a full application. Submit the pitch first via the NSF Seedfund portal.
