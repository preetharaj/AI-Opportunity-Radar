Google Cloud, in partnership with government innovation agencies across Southeast Asia, has launched its first dedicated AI accelerator for the region. The programme targets Seed to Series B startups building agentic AI products and connects them directly to Google engineers, cloud infrastructure, and international expansion pathways.

**Programme details:**
- Duration: 3 months (August–November 2026)
- Format: Hybrid — in-person boot camp in San Francisco or Singapore + remote sessions
- Cohort size: 25 startups (first batch)
- Equity-free — no ownership stake required

**What selected startups receive:**
- Direct engineering support from Google teams
- Google Cloud credits and TPU (Tensor Processing Unit) access for ML workloads
- Access to Gemini AI models and enterprise AI tooling
- In-person residency at Google's Mountain View and San Francisco campuses
- Technical boot camps and product development support
- Investor and enterprise customer introductions
- Support from EnterpriseSG (Singapore), Komdigi (Indonesia), NIC + SIHUB (Vietnam)

**Eligible countries:** Singapore, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand

**Key dates:**
- Applications close: June 20, 2026
- Notifications: Early August 2026
- Programme starts: Mid-August 2026
- Graduation: Early November 2026
