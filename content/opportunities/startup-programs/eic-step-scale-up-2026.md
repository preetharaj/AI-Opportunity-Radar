The EIC STEP Scale Up is a dedicated EU investment mechanism for companies in strategic technology sectors that are raising large growth rounds. Unlike the EIC Accelerator (which targets early/growth-stage), STEP Scale Up targets later-stage companies establishing market positions.

**Key parameters:**
- EIC co-investment: **€10M to €30M** per company
- Total funding round: **€50M to €150M** (EIC investment must be 3–5x smaller than total round)
- Pre-commitment required: A qualified investor must already commit to at least 20% of the total round
- Budget: **€300M** for 2026

**Eligible strategic sectors:**
- Artificial Intelligence and data
- Clean energy and climate technologies
- Advanced manufacturing and robotics
- Biotechnology and health technologies
- Space and connectivity
- Digital infrastructure and semiconductors

**2026 Deadlines:**
- ~~February 11~~ (passed)
- ~~May 6~~ (passed)
- **September 9, 2026** ← next deadline
- November 25, 2026

**What to submit:**
- Full business plan (max 50 pages) including ownership structure and STEP objectives
- Pitch deck (max 15 pages) in PDF
- Pre-commitment letter from a qualified investor (official EIC template, minimum 20% of round)

**Apply:** via the EU Funding & Tenders Portal at any time before a deadline. Applications are evaluated on innovation, strategic value, team, and scalability.
