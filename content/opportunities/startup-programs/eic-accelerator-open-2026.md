The EIC Accelerator Open is the European Innovation Council's flagship, sector-agnostic funding programme for high-risk, high-impact startups and SMEs. Unlike the thematic Challenges (e.g. GenAI4EU), the Open track accepts applications across any deep-tech domain.

- 4 November 2026

**Funding options:**
- **Grant only:** Up to €2.5M for innovation (TRL 6–8)
- **Blended finance:** Up to €2.5M grant + up to €10M equity (most common)
- **Equity only:** Available for TRL 9+ market deployment

**Key 2026 changes:**
- Full proposal reduced from 50 to 20 pages
- New Technology Expert Evaluator reviews each proposal
- Minimum equity component increased to €1M (up from €0.5M)
- 6 cut-off dates per year (up from 2 in previous years)

**Application process:**
1. **Step 1 (Short proposal):** 12 pages + 10 slides + 3-min video. Submit anytime — evaluated in 4–6 weeks.
2. **Step 2 (Full proposal):** 20 pages + pitch deck + budget. Submit before a cut-off date.
3. **Step 3 (Interview):** Live pitch to EIC jury in Brussels. Results in 2–3 weeks.

**Note:** Step 1 can be submitted at any time and is not tied to cut-off dates. To target the September 2 deadline for Step 2, submit Step 1 by end of July to allow evaluation time.
