Thinking Machines Lab — the AI research company founded by former OpenAI CTO Mira Murati — is awarding multiple $100,000 research grants for work that advances AI interactivity. Each grant also includes $25,000 in Tinker credits (their model fine-tuning platform).

**Research areas they're excited about:**

- **Evaluations for real-time multimodal interaction** — benchmarks for real-world real-time AI interaction quality
- **Explaining complex information with generative UI** — enabling AI to explain results via SVG, HTML, and other output formats so humans understand them
- **Steering agents during long-horizon tasks** — techniques for real-time human feedback and live steering of autonomous agents (training approaches + evals)

Not limited to these directions — any proposal advancing interactivity at scale is welcome.

**What to submit (email to [email protected]):**
- Research proposal (see apply page for full requirements)
- 1-page budget (institutional overhead ≤10% of cash grant)
- CVs of principal investigators and expected contributors
- Location and organisational details including tax ID

**Deadline:** June 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM PDT

**Note:** This grant was announced in mid-May 2026 — very new and not yet widely listed on grant aggregators. Verify the latest requirements directly at thinkingmachines.ai before submitting.
