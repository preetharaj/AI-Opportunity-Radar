F5 Foundation has officially opened applications for its 2026 STEM & AI Education Grant Program. Unlike previous cycles, the 2026 track makes **AI education mandatory** — every applicant must demonstrate how AI concepts, literacy, or competencies are integrated into their existing programmes.

**What's on offer:**
- 10 unrestricted grants of $50,000 each
- One-time funding — no strings attached on how it's spent
- Selected by a global committee of F5 employees

**Who can apply:**
- Registered nonprofits in Africa, Asia, or Latin America
- Must have operated for at least 3 years
- Primary beneficiaries must be individuals living in poverty (>50% of participants)
- AI integration is a mandatory programme component

**What they prioritise:**
- AI literacy education and use of AI tools
- Quantitative evidence of programme effectiveness
- Strong measurable impact on STEM access

**For nonprofits outside the US:** An Enhanced Expenditure Responsibility (EER) review is required, handled through F5's partner CAF America.

**Deadline: June 14, 2026 at 11:45 PM PST**
