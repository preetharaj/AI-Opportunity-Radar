Thinking Machines Lab offers two rolling grant types for academics using their Tinker fine-tuning platform:

**Teaching Grants — $250 per student:**
- For academic classes integrating Tinker into coursework
- Sized to support a full class for a course duration
- Works for assignments or self-directed student projects

**Research Grants — starting at $5,000:**
- For researchers training open-weight LLMs
- Rolling assessment — responses within ~1 week
- No fixed deadline; apply whenever ready

**Who's been funded:** Stanford, CMU, and various research labs.

Apply via the Thinking Machines website. Separate application forms for research and teaching grants.
